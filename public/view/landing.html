What is TalkED?

Talked is a video conferencing tool powered by WebRTC. It is designed to provide a more interactive online class discussions with the use of polls for questions during discussions and word cloud for sharing insights about a certain topic. Share your screen for discussions or use whiteboard that is as if itâ€™s a physical whiteboard like in the classroom setting and record your meetings with the consent of the participants so that students can playback the discussions.

TalkED was made because online class discussions lacked interaction between the instructors and the students. The developers of TalkED were students who experienced having an online class and because of that, they knew and they researched what should be improved. This inspired them to create a video conferencing tool that has features that can make the students participate more.

About Us

TalkED was made because its developers were students and they are currently using a video conferencing tool for their online learning. They were inspired to make a tool for online learning where they added features that can help both students and professors for their online discussions.They aimed to provide a more interactive and lively discussions while also having a interface that is easy to use and is accessible in pc and mobile.

Features

Share Screen

Share a specific application window, chrome tab or your whole screen for your discussions or presentations.

Whiteboard

Have a clear discussions using whiteboard where you can write just like a real whiteboard.

Wordcloud

Know the insights of your students or classmates where the most answered word or phrase will be displayed in a bigger font size.

Record

Record your meetings and download it to playback your discussions.

Chat

Communicate and chat your questions or answers in the chat box.

Polls

Ask a question and make a poll to make it more interactive.

Show Participants

See all the participants in your meetings.

File Sharing

Share your files in the chat box.
